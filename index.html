The plan

The long distance is finally coming to an end! For logistical and financial reasons, Matt and Vivian will be having 3 "weddings": A lunch reception in Malaysia, a civil ceremony in Halifax, NS, and a family get-together in Napanee, ON, in the summer of 2020. If you have pre-existing notions of what weddings are supposed to be like, scratch that. The bride & groom prefer keeping things simple. This website will serve as a compilation of our journey, and we will link pictures and videos here for those who can't be in attendance.

Events

22nd August, 2020

Ontario reception 1PM onwards

We are throwing a little party at Matt's parent's backyard on Hay Bay in Greater Napanee. Details to follow.

12th October, 2019

Civil ceremony 2PM - 3PM

This is where we made things official! Connie Miller is Matt's boss, a dear friend, and our Justice of the Peace. We had an intimate ceremony attended by close family members at Victoria Park in Halifax. After the ceremony, we walked over to Le Bistro by Liz for lunch.

14th September, 2019

Tea ceremony 敬茶仪式 11AM - 12PM

This important Chinese tradition is where the bride and groom serves tea to family members. Matt got to address family members by their honorifics, in Mandarin and Teochew! In return, family give the newlyweds their blessings (and red packets).

Lunch reception 12PM - 3PM

This is as Chinese as it gets! Complete with a fake wedding cake and lots of yummy food. The event was beautifully captured on camera by our photographer Anna. Check out our photos HERE.
